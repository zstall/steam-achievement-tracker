How It Works

1

Connect Your Steam Account

Securely link your Steam account using Steam's official Web API. We fetch your game library, achievements, and playtime data to provide personalized experiences.

2

Create Custom Achievements

Design achievements tailored to your gaming style. Set completion conditions, choose game collections, and add custom images to make them uniquely yours.

3

Track & Share Progress

Monitor your progress in real-time as our system automatically tracks your Steam data. Share achievements with the community and discover new challenges.

4

Connect & Compete

Build your gaming network, follow friends' achievements, and participate in community challenges to enhance your gaming experience.
